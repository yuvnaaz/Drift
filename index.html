The Way of Drift

Guide the paper boat to the warm dock lantern by painting gentle wind currents.

  • Paint Wind: Click and drag on the parchment to paint a watercolor brush stroke of wind. The direction of your drag guides the breeze.
  • Deselect / Erase: Click a painted stroke to select it, then click its red **x** handle to erase it.
  • Cast Off: Press **Cast Off** to release the boat. Watch it drift naturally. All menus will fade to let you meditate on its journey.
  • Recall & Tweak: Click **Recall Boat** at any time to return to the easel and adjust your strokes.

Tip: Watch the cherry blossom petals and water ripples. They reveal the hidden flow of wind and water.
